What do you need help on?

"We simply attempt to be fearful when others are greedy and to be greedy only when others are fearful." - Warren Buffet

Spend less than what you have and save as much as possible

Budget out your spending and don’t spend more than you planned

Don’t take any loans or get any debt

If you have debt, pay it off as soon as possible

Invest into stocks so that you make money without having to work (invest while the stock is low)

Put your money into a bank account and use interest to make money

Don’t buy things with money that you don’t have

Find a job that will pay you money (example: chores)
