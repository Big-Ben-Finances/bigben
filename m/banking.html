Banking

Banking is like saving, but it is when you put your money into a bank. It is safer than your piggy bank and makes you some money because of interest.

WHAT IS A BANK?

A bank is a place where you put your money and it is protected by experts.



Benny puts in $10 into his bank account. Since the bank gives him interest, he has $11 by the end of the year. He makes $1 because he puts his money in the bank.

"I THOUGHT INTEREST IS BAD!"

This type of interest is different. Instead of paying the bank, the bank pays you. This will make you some money without having to work.

If you want to make easy money, ask your parents to create a bank account for you. Whenever you want to buy something, you can take it out of your account. When you don’t, you get to make money!
