Interest



When you go to the bank and get a loan, you will have to pay interest on the amount that you got.

WHAT IS INTEREST?

Interest is when you have to pay back some extra borrowed money to the person who gives it to you.



Benny goes to the store and sees a teddy bear he wants to buy. The teddy bear costs $10. He only brought $7. He can get a loan from his mom for $3. He will have to pay back the $3 that he owes to the mom and a little more because of interest ($4).

Interest will grow over time slowly. The longer you don’t pay back your loans, the more money you need to pay back later. Try not to take loans and just look at the toys that you can buy.
