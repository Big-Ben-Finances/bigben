Budgeting

What if there was a way to plan your money spending so that you don’t spend it all? There is a way to do that! That is called budgeting.

WHAT IS BUDGETING?

Budgeting is a way to manage your money over a period of time.

HOW CAN YOU MAKE A BUDGET?

You can make spreadsheets putting all your costs and what you made into the little squares. Than you can add them up and see how much you saved. You can also get a piece of paper and add up your losses and gains.



Benny makes $10 every week. Benny plans to have at least $2 in savings this week. Benny write a budget plan. He knows that $10-$2 is $8, so he can only spend $8 on toys and candy. Benny likes toys more so he spends $5 for toys and $3 for candy.

A budget is always great for managing your expenses. The next time you don’t know what to do with your money, put it in a budget.
