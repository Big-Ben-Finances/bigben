Invest

You can make money without doing chores. The way you can do that is by investing!

WHAT IS INVESTING?

Investing is when you give money to companies so you can own a small piece of it. This is called buying stocks. Not all stocks make money, though. If the company's stock price drops then you lose money.

Benny wants to buy the latest HotWheels car, but he doesn’t want to do chores. He invested $5 into the Pear® company. Since the company made money, they pay him $10 the next week. With the money he just made, he buys the toy car set.

WHEN SHOULD I INVEST?

Try investing when the stock market is really low. When the stock prices rise, you will make a lot of money.
