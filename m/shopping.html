Shopping

Buying new things is always really fun and exciting, but it is better to buy them when they have a discount.

WHAT ARE DISCOUNTS?

Discounts are when the seller wants to sell something for less.



Benny wants to buy a toy car for $5. When he goes to the store, he sees that it has a discount and is selling for only $4. He buys the toy and saves $1.

As you can see, discounts are a good way to save money. Next time you go to the store, try to find things you like that have discounts on them.

Another thing you can use to save money are coupons. Unlike discounts, you need to look for coupons (usually online).

WHAT ARE COUPONS?

Coupons are like tickets that can give you discounts.

Benny wants to buy a toy car for $5. When he goes to the store, he brings his coupon that makes things $1 off. He buys the toy for $4 and saves $1.

Just like Benny, you can save your money when you want to buy something by using discounts or coupons. Buy smartly!
