Washington on Work

The first way that most people earn money is by working.

WHAT IS WORK? Work is when you do something to get a payment in return. For example, chores are an example of work. Every time you do the dishes, you get money for doing the work.

EXAMPLE: Benny wants to earn some money, so he asks his parents if he can work. They tells him that he has to clean the pool for $5 every week. Whenever Benny cleans the pool, they give him $5.]

Some examples of work are:
- Taking out the trash
- Doing the laundry
- Washing dishes
- Sweeping/vacuuming
Anything where you do something for another thing in return is called working.
