Benjamin on Retirement

The end goal for most people is to retire.

WHAT IS RETIREMENT? It is when you have enough money so that you don’t need to work anymore. You save up so much money that you can live without having to work.

HOW DOES RETIREMENT WORK? When you retire, you stop getting money (except money that comes from stocks, houses, or other investments). How do you maintain your lifestyle if you don’t get money? The government gives you the money that you saved up in the bank every month, so you can still pay for things you want or need.

HOW DO YOU RETIRE? You can retire by setting aside some of your money. The money can be used to keep up the way you live, even when you’re not working.

EXAMPLE: Benny has worked for a long time and he saves a part of the money that he has made. So, he retires. Every month, he takes money from his bank account and uses it to buy food and the things he wants.
