Jackson on Debt

Today, you can get anything you want even if you don’t have enough money to buy it. That is called debt and it sounds cool, but it is the worst thing that can happen to your piggy bank.

WHAT IS DEBT? Debt is when the bank lets you borrow their money for more in return.

WHY IS DEBT SUCH A BIG DEAL? Debt makes money off of people with time pressure and people that don’t have enough money to buy something. Once you’re in debt, it takes a long time to get out. Sometimes it takes people years to get out of debt because they get so much of it. The bank wants to make as much money as possible, so they spread out the debt payments and charge more interest, which makes them more money.

So the next time you want to get something that you can’t afford, saving is the way to go. Don’t spend money that you don't actually have.
